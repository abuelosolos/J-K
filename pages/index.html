How it works

Three steps to getting paid

01 — Create
Generate an invoice
Set the job title and amount in USD. Fara instantly generates a unique payment link you can send to any client, anywhere in the world.
02 — Collect
Client pays by card
Your client opens the link and pays with their debit or credit card. No account needed on their end. Fara deducts a flat $2 fee and converts the rest to NGN.
03 — Withdraw
Cash out to your bank
Funds land in your Fara wallet instantly after payment. Withdraw to any Nigerian bank account with a single click. Arrives within 24 hours.

FAQ

Common questions

When a client pays your invoice, Fara deducts a flat $2 fee from the amount. The remainder is converted to NGN at the live exchange rate and credited to your Fara wallet. For example, a $500 invoice means you receive the equivalent of $498 in NGN.
Withdrawals are processed within 1 business day. Funds are sent directly to your Nigerian bank account via Interswitch. You'll need to complete KYC verification before your first withdrawal.
KYC (Know Your Customer) is required by the Central Bank of Nigeria for any platform handling financial transactions. We collect your BVN and NIN to verify your identity. This protects you and keeps Fara compliant. Verification typically takes 1–2 business days.
No. Your client only needs the payment link you send them. They enter their name, email, and card details — that's it. They don't need to sign up for anything. If they want to track payments they can optionally create a client account.
Fara uses the current market rate for USD to NGN conversion. The rate is displayed on your dashboard and on each payment. We do not add a spread or markup to the exchange rate — you get the full amount at the live rate, minus only the $2 flat fee.
